RASPBERRY PI

This is me: the Raspberry Pi B. Overclocked to 950Mhz
and equipped with 512 MB SDRAM it’s my brain in a way.
When I do a lot of thinking it’s getting freaking hot
in here. Most of the time I feel cold however. Brrrrrrrr!

ANALOG-DIGITAL CONVERTER

Huh? That sounds weird to my mind… It’s a good thing that the guys of denkwerk implemented this little component: the MCP3008 Analog to Digital Converter.
It samples signals 200.000 times per second and translates all that analog gibberish into comprehensible instructions for me. A-A-A-A-A-Aight!

WI-FI MODULE

The wire? Heard about, but never seen. With this Wi-Fi adapter I encrypt WPA2 and WEP and go on the internet without any wires. I feel as free as a bird – Hoooooooooray!

SOUNDCARD

This is my SpeedLink Soundcard with a sampling rate
of 48K – responsible not only to my sharp sense
of hearing, but my feeling for language as well.
Dude, it’s fair to say I’m an allrounder. Isn’t it?

SERVO MOTOR

This tiny unit is my PowerPro MG90S Servo Motor with 1.8kg/cm to 2.2kg/cm power. Knowing him was a real eye-opener to me. That’s why I got even two of them.
Just look into my eyes and convince yourself. Wink, wink!

LOUDSPEAKERS

You better watch your mouth. Because my Visaton K 45 –
8 Ohm repeats every singlesound you deliver – with 2W
in a transmission range (-10 dB) between 300 – 14000 Hz.
So don’t blame me for your funny pronunciation.
You started it!

BATTERY

Pooh! Such a working day can be so energy-sapping.
I’m glad that I have this Anker external battery with
a capacity of 10000mAh 3.7v 37Wh. This is the place where I take my power from when I’m zonked.
The show mustgo on, right?

MICROPHONE

Haha – good one. With my CZ034 Electret Microphone
I can record all noises in the range between -32dB ± 3dB.
It doesn’t matter whether it‘s a funny joke or the latest rumours. I lend an ear to you. Microphone check!
One, two.
